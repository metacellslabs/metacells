# Sponsors

MetaCells is an open project developed in the open.

The project explores new computational models for automation, AI workflows, and system design based on a cell-oriented architecture.

Development of such infrastructure requires sustained research, experimentation, and engineering effort. Sponsorship helps support this work.

## Founding sponsors

Early development of the project was supported by friendly companies and early adopters who believe in open infrastructure and programmable systems.

# Why sponsorship matters

Sponsorship allows the project to continue developing as open infrastructure.

Support helps fund:

- research and experimentation
- development of the core architecture
- documentation and examples
- infrastructure and maintenance
- long-term stability of the project

MetaCells aims to remain open and accessible to developers, researchers, and organizations.
